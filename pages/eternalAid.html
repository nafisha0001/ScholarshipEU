    The following criteria based on percentage of marks obtained in the qualifying examination will be applicable during the first semester only for the newly admitted UG & PG students for the Academic Session 2021-22 subject to payment of full Tuition Fee for a semester or for a year:
    2. 95% and above 75% of the Tuition Fee
    90% to < 95% 50% of the Tuition Fee
    80% to < 90% 30% of the Tuition Fee
    70% to < 80% 10% of the Tuition Fee
  2. The tuition fee waiver rules:
    For the real two sisters, it will be as under: Till the period both the sisters studying in the University : 50% tuition fee will be waived off to the first elder sister. Once the first sister passes out from the University : 100% tuition fee will be paid by the second sister till the completion of the course.
  3. 100% tuition fee waiver for the duration of degree course to the students whose parents (Both Mother & Father) have lost their life due to Covid-19 pandemic.
  4. 25% tuition fee waiver for the duration of degree course will also be applicable for the following categories:
    • Wards of Defense personnel i.e. (Army/Air Force/Navy) both either serving or retired.
    • Wards of the Freedom Fighters.
    • Single girl child.

However, such student as categorized in clause (C) & (D) above, at the time of admission and in consecutive semester/years of the degree course will not be entitled for other scholarship on the merit basis as approved earlier.
